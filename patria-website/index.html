Technology focus

Our work focuses on the development, validation and testing of composite hydrogen storage systems in line with European safety standards and industrial requirements.

Type IV composite vessels

Development of high-pressure Type IV hydrogen storage vessels with emphasis on structural integrity, permeation control and lifecycle performance.

Testing & validation

Proof, burst and cyclic testing in cooperation with certified partners, with the objective of establishing in-house testing capabilities.

R&D and EU projects

Active participation in European and national R&D initiatives supporting hydrogen deployment, pilot systems and testing infrastructure.

About Patria Composite

Patria Composite d.o.o. is a technology-oriented company focused on composite hydrogen storage vessels and related testing infrastructure.

The company operates at the intersection of industrial development, applied research and European R&D projects, supporting the deployment of hydrogen technologies across different use cases.
