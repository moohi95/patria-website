About Patria Composite

Patria Composite d.o.o. is an engineering and development company from Samobor, Croatia, focused on composite hydrogen storage vessels and supporting testing infrastructure.

We participate in EU and national R&D projects and collaborate with industrial and academic partners across the EU to support deployment of green hydrogen.

Our scope includes Type IV vessels, testing systems, and integration in mobility, industry and stationary applications.
